Age Calculator – Free Age Calculator Online

Age Calculator is a free, beautifully designed age calculator that computes your exact age in years, months, days, hours, minutes, and live seconds — all from your date of birth. It is ideal for job applications, school admissions, passport forms, medical records, and everyday curiosity.

Beyond basic age calculation, our Age Calculator shows your next birthday countdown, your zodiac sign with personality traits, a beautiful shareable age card, and personalised gift ideas — all instantly, securely, and privately in your browser.

What is an Age Calculator?

An Age Calculator determines your exact age from your date of birth. It computes the precise difference in years, months, days, and even seconds between two dates, giving you instant and accurate results for any official or personal need.

How accurate is this Age Calculator?

100% accurate. The calculator accounts for leap years, varying month lengths, and calculates in real-time down to the live second.

Can I share my Age Card?

Yes! After calculating, scroll down to your personalised Age Card and use the WhatsApp, Twitter, Facebook, or Copy Link buttons to share it instantly.

Is my data stored or shared?

Never. All calculations happen entirely in your browser. No personal data is collected, stored, or sent to any server.
