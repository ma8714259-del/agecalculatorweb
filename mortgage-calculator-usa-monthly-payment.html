Example: $300,000 Home in USA

30-year fixed mortgage at 7% interest with 20% down payment ($60,000):

  • Loan amount: $240,000
  • Monthly principal and interest: $1,597
  • Property taxes (estimate): $300/month
  • Homeowner's insurance: $100/month
  • Total monthly payment: approximately $1,997

The 20% down payment rule helps you avoid Private Mortgage Insurance (PMI), which adds $50 to $200 per month to your payment. If you put down less than 20%, factor in PMI costs.

First-Time Home Buyer Programs in USA

  • FHA Loans — As low as 3.5% down payment
  • USDA Loans — Zero down payment for rural areas
  • VA Loans — Zero down for veterans
  • Fannie Mae HomeReady — 3% down for low-income buyers
  • State programs — Many US states offer additional grants

Steps to Get a Mortgage in USA

  1. Check and improve your credit score (aim for 620+ minimum, 740+ for best rates)
  2. Save for down payment and closing costs (2% to 5% of loan amount)
  3. Get pre-approved from at least 3 lenders
  4. Find your home and make an offer
  5. Complete the full mortgage application
  6. Home inspection and appraisal
  7. Close the loan and move in
