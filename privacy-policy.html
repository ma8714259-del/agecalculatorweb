Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

Welcome to Age Calculator ("we", "us", or "our"), accessible at https://agecalculatorweb.com. Your privacy is extremely important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our website.

1. Information We Collect

We do NOT collect any personal information. The date of birth you enter into our calculator is processed entirely within your browser and is never sent to our servers. We do not store, save, or transmit your date of birth or any calculation results.

2. How the Calculator Works

All calculations performed on this website happen locally in your browser using JavaScript. This means:

  • Your date of birth never leaves your device
  • No data is sent to any server
  • No database stores your information
  • You can use this tool with complete peace of mind

3. Cookies

Our website does not use tracking cookies or any cookies that identify you personally. We may use basic session data only to ensure the website functions correctly in your browser.

4. Third-Party Services

Our website uses Google Fonts to display typography. Google may collect limited technical data as per their own privacy policy. We do not use any advertising networks, tracking pixels, or analytics services that collect personal data.

5. Google AdSense

We may display advertisements through Google AdSense. Google may use cookies to show relevant ads based on your browsing history. You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings.

6. Children's Privacy

Our service is safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Since we collect no personal data at all, children can safely use our calculator.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please visit our Contact page and send us a message. We will respond within 24–48 hours.
