What Your Zodiac Sign Says About Your Personality

May 2026  ·  6 min read  ·  By Age Calculator Team

Your date of birth determines your zodiac sign, and for millions of people around the world, their zodiac sign is a meaningful part of their identity. The 12 signs of the Western zodiac are each associated with distinct personality traits, strengths, and tendencies. Whether you are a firm believer or a curious sceptic, reading about your sign is always a fascinating experience.

The 12 Zodiac Signs and Their Traits

♈

Aries

March 21 – April 19

Bold, ambitious and energetic. Aries are natural leaders who love a challenge and dive headfirst into any situation. They are passionate, motivated, and confident — with a competitive streak that drives them to always be first.

♉

Taurus

April 20 – May 20

Reliable, patient and practical. Taurus values stability, comfort and beauty, and is one of the most dependable signs. They are hardworking, devoted, and love the finer things in life — good food, comfort, and lasting relationships.

♊

Gemini

May 21 – June 20

Curious, adaptable and outgoing. Geminis are known for their quick wit, versatility and love of conversation. They can see multiple sides of every issue, making them great communicators and fascinating companions.

♋

Cancer

June 21 – July 22

Intuitive, emotional and nurturing. Cancer is deeply connected to home and family, and is one of the most empathetic signs. They are highly sensitive, protective of those they love, and have powerful instincts.

♌

Leo

July 23 – August 22

Charismatic, warm and creative. Leos are natural performers who love being in the spotlight and inspiring others. They are generous, confident, and have a magnetic personality that draws people toward them.

♍

Virgo

August 23 – September 22

Analytical, kind and hardworking. Virgos have a keen eye for detail and a deep desire to be helpful. They are practical, methodical, and reliable — often the person others turn to for solid, grounded advice.

♎

Libra

September 23 – October 22

Diplomatic, fair-minded and social. Libra is the sign of balance and justice. They are charming, cooperative, and hate conflict — always striving to create harmony in their relationships and surroundings.

♏

Scorpio

October 23 – November 21

Passionate, resourceful and brave. Scorpios are determined and decisive, known for their intensity and depth. They are fiercely loyal and protective of those they love, and possess remarkable intuition.

♐

Sagittarius

November 22 – December 21

Adventurous, optimistic and freedom-loving. Sagittarius loves to explore — both physically and intellectually. They are enthusiastic, open-minded, and have a wonderful sense of humour and an unquenchable thirst for knowledge.

♑

Capricorn

December 22 – January 19

Disciplined, responsible and self-controlled. Capricorns are ambitious and determined, with a strong sense of duty. They are practical, patient, and excel at long-term planning — often achieving remarkable success through perseverance.

♒

Aquarius

January 20 – February 18

Progressive, original and independent. Aquarius is the visionary of the zodiac. They are intellectual, humanitarian, and often ahead of their time — with a unique perspective that challenges conventional thinking.

♓

Pisces

February 19 – March 20

Compassionate, artistic and intuitive. Pisces are deeply empathetic and creative, with a rich inner world. They are gentle, wise, and often have a profound connection to music, art, and the emotions of those around them.
