About Age Calculator

Age Calculator is a free privacy-focused tool that helps people calculate their exact age in years, months, days, hours, minutes and seconds. We also offer a free BMI Calculator and Loan EMI Calculator.

Our calculators are used by people around the world for visa applications, official documents, health checks and everyday curiosity. Everything runs in your browser and no data is ever sent to our servers.
