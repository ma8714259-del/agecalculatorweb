How to Reduce Your Loan EMI in Pakistan

High EMI putting pressure on your budget? Here are proven ways to lower it:

  1. Make a larger down payment — less principal means lower EMI directly
  2. Choose a longer tenure — spreads payments over more months (but total interest increases)
  3. Negotiate the interest rate — especially if you have your salary account with that bank
  4. Improve your eCIB score — a clean credit history gets better rates from banks
  5. Apply when SBP rate is falling — timing matters for variable-rate loans
  6. Compare at least 3 banks — even 1% difference saves PKR 50,000–200,000 on a large loan
  7. Consider Islamic finance — Meezan Bank and others offer competitive Shariah-compliant options
