What is a Personal Loan Interest Rate?

A personal loan interest rate is the percentage of the loan amount charged by the lender each year for borrowing money. It directly determines how much your monthly repayment will be.

Fixed vs Variable Interest Rates

TypeWhat ChangesBest When
Fixed RateMonthly payment stays the sameInterest rates are rising

What Determines Your Personal Loan Interest Rate?

  • Credit Score — Higher score = lower rate
  • Income Level — Higher income = more trust from lenders
  • Loan Amount — Very small and very large loans may have higher rates
  • Loan Duration — Longer terms often have higher rates
  • Employment Type — Salaried employees often get better rates than self-employed

How to Qualify for a Lower Interest Rate

  1. Improve your credit score by paying bills on time
  2. Reduce your existing debt before applying
  3. Apply with a co-borrower or guarantor
  4. Choose a shorter loan duration
  5. Compare offers from at least 3 to 5 lenders
