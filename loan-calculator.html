Loan Calculator – Free Multi-Currency Loan Calculator Online

Our free Loan Calculator supports USD, GBP, EUR, CAD, AUD, PKR, INR, AED, SAR and more. Instantly calculate your monthly payment, total interest, and complete yearly repayment schedule for any home loan, car loan, or personal loan.

Simply select your currency, enter the loan amount, interest rate, and term — and get accurate results in seconds. All calculations happen privately in your browser with no data stored.

How is the monthly payment calculated?

Using the standard amortization formula: M = P[r(1+r)^n]/[(1+r)^n−1], where P is principal, r is monthly rate, and n is total months. This gives the fixed monthly payment to fully repay your loan.

Which currencies are supported?

USD (US Dollar), GBP (British Pound), EUR (Euro), CAD (Canadian Dollar), AUD (Australian Dollar), PKR (Pakistani Rupee), INR (Indian Rupee), AED (UAE Dirham), and SAR (Saudi Riyal).

Is my financial data stored?

Never. All calculations run entirely in your browser. No personal or financial data is collected, stored, or transmitted. Your privacy is fully protected.

Can I use this for a mortgage or car loan?

Yes! This calculator works for all loan types — mortgages, car loans, personal loans, student loans, and business loans — in any supported currency.
