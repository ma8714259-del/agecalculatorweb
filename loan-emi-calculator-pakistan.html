Types of Loans Available in Pakistan

  • Personal Loans — For home renovation, weddings, medical emergencies, education
  • Car Loans — For new or used vehicles. Banks finance 70–85% of vehicle value
  • Home Loans / Mortgages — For buying or building a house. Tenures up to 20–25 years
  • Business Loans — For SMEs and entrepreneurs
  • Mera Pakistan Mera Ghar — Government subsidized housing scheme with lower rates
  • Islamic Finance — Shariah-compliant options: Murabaha, Ijarah, Diminishing Musharakah

Tips to Reduce Your EMI in Pakistan

  • Make a larger down payment — reduces principal, directly lowers EMI
  • Improve your credit score — check your eCIB report; clean history gets better rates
  • Choose a longer tenure — lowers monthly EMI (but increases total interest)
  • Negotiate with your bank — especially if you have salary account with them
  • Compare multiple banks — even 1–2% difference saves lakhs over loan lifetime
  • Watch the SBP rate — apply when policy rate is falling for better rates
