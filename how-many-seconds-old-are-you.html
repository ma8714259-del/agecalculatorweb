How Many Seconds Old Are You? The Fascinating Math of Age

May 2026 · 4 min read · By Age Calculator Team

Have you ever wondered how many seconds you have been alive? If you are 25 years old, you have lived over 788 million seconds. At 31 years and 8 months, you cross the one-billion-second milestone. These numbers make the abstract concept of time feel beautifully concrete.

The Basic Calculation

One year contains approximately 31,536,000 seconds. A leap year adds another 86,400 seconds. So every four years, your total second count gets an extra boost — which is why our calculator accounts for every leap year in your lifetime to give a truly accurate live count.

Milestone Ages in Seconds

  • Age 1: about 31.5 million seconds
  • Age 10: about 315 million seconds
  • Age 25: about 788 million seconds
  • Age 31 years 8 months: one billion seconds
  • Age 50: about 1.58 billion seconds
  • Age 100: about 3.15 billion seconds
